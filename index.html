OUR CORE WORK

Oral disease is one of the world’s most pervasive health issues, yet it is one of the most overlooked and under-publicized.

We’re solving global oral health challenges locally to deliver lasting impact to disadvantaged communities in Costa Rica and eventually in the entire American Hemisphere

Through Our Global Network, We Offer:

  • Oral health education through school-based partnerships

  • A Fellowship that empowers local youth through sustainable careers in dentistry, and

  • Access to oral health care services for disadvantaged communities that would not otherwise have the opportunity to be treated by a dentist in their lifetime. 8 YEARS OF SUSTAINABLE IMPACT Learn More

We have helped bring healthier smiles to over 10,000 children in 9 countries across 4 continents--in Haiti, India, Romania, El Salvador, Guatemala, Iraq, the Dominican Republic, Costa Rica, and the United States.

However, due to the pandemic, we have shifted our focus from global to the Americas starting in Costa Rica.
